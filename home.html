About Us

Robin

Raafiq

It all began one day when the co-founder, Robin had returned from a trip to Ankara, the capital of Turkey. He had enjoyed the delicacies there and wanted to bring his friends to experience it again in Singapore.

However, there were only a few restaurants scattered across the country and they were in hard to reach locations.

This was why he started Food Fighters - to deliver delicious turkish dishes to the customer's doorstep anytime in the day.

And through this, we hope more people would come to love turkish food just as much as we do!
